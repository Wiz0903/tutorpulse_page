# TutorPulse Website

Official landing page for **TutorPulse** — an offline-first Android app built for community tutors in South Africa to track attendance, manage learners, record payments, and share reports with parents — all without internet.

## Why It Exists

Many tutors still rely on paper registers or scattered notes. This makes it hard to:
- Track who attends consistently  
- Identify learners who need extra help  
- Show parents clear progress  

TutorPulse replaces paper with a simple, reliable mobile tool that works even during load-shedding or on budget Android phones (R1,000+).

## Key Features (App Highlights)

- **Fully offline** – No internet, login, or account required  
- **Role-based access** – Admins manage data; tutors mark attendance only  
- **Attendance insights** – See each learner’s % attendance and last session  
- **One-tap export** – Share CSV reports directly via WhatsApp  
- **Payment tracking** – Record and monitor learner payments  
- **Built for reality** – Tested with 4 active tutors in Bloemfontein
